Question 1

Quit India Movement is also known as

  1. August Movement
  2. September Movement
  3. June Movement
  4. None of the above

The Correct Answer is :-

Option A: August Movement

Question 2

Who was the Prime Minister of Britain when the Quit India Movement started?

  1. Roosevelt
  2. Churchill
  3. Mountbatten
  4. Simon

The Correct Answer is :-

Option B: Churchill

Question 3

Who had given the slogan of “Do or Die” during the Quit India Movement?

  1. Jawarharlal Nehru
  2. Sardar Patel
  3. BR Ambedkar
  4. Mahatma Gandhi

The Correct Answer is :-

Option D: Mahatma Gandhi

Question 5
